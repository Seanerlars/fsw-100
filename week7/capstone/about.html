Meet Our Team

Dennis Reynolds

Dennis Reynolds is co-owner and bartender at Carmines. He brings his positive attitude and intense energy everywhere he goes. When he is not working on serving you a Carmine’s signature cocktail, he is making his rounds around our restaurant floor to see that everyone is satisfied. Dennis co-founded Carmines in February of 2015 with the intention of finding the perfect chicken recipe for a new prestigious eatery. Upon discussing with his colleagues, Dennis shifted his focus on making and serving the most delicious steaks in the area. Be sure to say hello as Dennis prides himself on his sociability and fun demeanor.

Mac

Mac will very likely be the very first person you meet at the door as he is head of security at Carmines. Of course, Carmines is a family restaurant during weekday hours, but at night the restaurant becomes a hot spot for those seeking a fun and relaxed nightlife bar scene. Mac has an impressive list of combat credentials. Mac is a blue belt in karate and an entrepreneur with his own brand of protein shake called “fight milk.” Despite his extensive background as a rule enforcer, Mac considers himself to be friendly and kind to others.

Dee Reynolds

Dee Reynolds is co-owner and hostess here at Carmines. A former aspiring actress, Dee can do just about anything. Don’t be fooled though, her clever acting and impressions have made it clear that not any one person knows exactly who she is. Dee’s personality is at the heart of what makes Carmines special. Her wittiness and charm will have you coming back for more.

Charlie

Meet Charlie. An expert on cleanliness and the janitorial arts. Charlie is in charge of making sure your dining experience is as pleasant as it gets. You will not be able to find a stain anywhere you go at Carmines because of this man. His skills range from dishwashing, bathroom cleaning, bussing, and landscaping. If Dee Reynolds is the heart of Carmine’s, then Charlie is the hands. His ability to spot and clean messes is unmatched, and his drive to get the job done is an example to servicemen and women everywhere. If you see Charlie mopping the floor or dusting the chandeliers be sure to give him a firm handshake for his hard work, or “Charlie-work” as he likes to call it.

Frank Reynolds

Frank Reynolds is the primary owner of Carmines. His former experience running his own wall-street business put things into perspective for frank: that money isn’t everything. Instead, Frank decided to open up a steak house with his two kids and he hasn’t looked back. Frank is not as hands-on as the other staff but focuses on the logistics of the restaurant in order for Carmines to operate as efficient as possible. Frank has gathered a fantastic staff of people in order to serve YOU, and his heart and soul goes into serving the great people of California.
