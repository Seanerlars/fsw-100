Who are We

This beautiful rake has seen its days in the yard. Moderately used but still full of heart. This rake can change your life. Money can buy things brand new, but it cannot buy experience... Except in this case it can. Here at Broken Treasures we take man-made tools that have seen their time, and adopt them to new homes (for a small fee). We believe in finding the right families for all used and broken inanimate objects. Looking to throw items away? Check out our adoption center. We take in hundred of thousands of millions, of used or broken items. People travel from all over the country to give us these beautiful items, and although we have yet to find a single home for our broken items, and most people think we're a landfill (we are not) our message is set on finding all these beatiful Broken Treasures their new homes.
